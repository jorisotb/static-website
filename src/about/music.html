Music

This is a taste of my music taste, as indicated by what I believe to be the best, or most interesting albums. I have restricted myself to a single album per artist, the list would become far too long otherwise. There's already 39 albums on it as it is.

Check out my Last.fm page for more.

Opeth - Blackwater Park (2001)
Progressive Metal
The best album ever. Listening to this in a Swedish forest changed me.
Dream Theater - Metropolis pt2: Scenes from a Memory (1999)
Progressive Metal
A story about reincarnation by some of the best musicians prog-metal has to offer.
The Ocean Collective - Pelagial (2013)
Progressive/Death Metal
The songs are named after oceanic depth zones and get heavier as you go deeper. Peak concept album.
Haken - The Mountain (2013)
Progressive Metal
This album is somehow stereotypical prog and utterly unique at the same time.
Ghost - Meliora (2015)
Progressive/Psychedelic Rock
Many bands make concept albums. Ghost is a concept band.
Dool - Summerland (2020)
Progressive/Psychedelic Rock
Why is an album from 2020 making me feel this nostalgic?
Year of the Goat - Novis Orbis Terrarum Ordinis (2019)
Progressive/Psychedelic Rock
50% fun and upbeat, 50% calm and moving, 100% intriguing.

Pink Floyd - The Wall (1979)
